Good to know

Frequently asked questions

What should I expect at my first visit?+

Your first visit includes a comprehensive examination with X-rays and photos, plus a professional clean. Our dentists assess your oral health, discuss any concerns and provide a tailored treatment plan.

How does the no gap check-up and clean work?+

If you're a new patient with a participating health fund, your check-up and clean, including your comprehensive exam, scale and clean, and fluoride, is covered with no out-of-pocket gap. Eligibility depends on your level of cover. T&Cs apply.

What if I don't have private health cover?+

No problem. We offer an affordable new-patient exam and clean package that includes a full exam, X-rays, photos, professional clean and fluoride, and we also offer interest-free payment plans.

Do you offer emergency and Saturday appointments?+

Yes. We keep time for dental emergencies and provide same-day care where possible. We're open Monday to Friday and offer Saturday appointments by request. Call (02) 4234 0200.

Is teeth whitening safe?+

We use professional-grade Zoom whitening, which is safe and effective and can brighten your teeth several shades in a single visit. Individual results vary.

At what age should my child first see a dentist?+

It's recommended to bring your child for their first dental visit by their first birthday, or within six months of their first tooth appearing, to monitor development and build healthy habits early.
